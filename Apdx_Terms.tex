
\chapter{Physical and Chemical Terms}
\label{chap:phys-chem-terms}

An ion's {\bf valence} is the number of charges, plus or minus, that
it carries. So, \ce{Ca2+} is a {\it divalent}, while \ce{K+} is a {\it
  univalent}.
